Meet the Developers

Hannan Shahzad

Hannan Shahzad is an 11-year-old tech enthusiast with a love for learning and creativity. He recently completed a course in HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap at the Ali Jiwani Institute for Professional Development (IPD), where he began his exciting journey into web development. Hannan played a key role in designing and developing the Better Tomorrow website, using his new skills to bring the vision to life. He enjoys exploring how websites work, trying out new design ideas, and solving problems with code. Hannan is passionate about using technology to build meaningful projects and hopes to create innovative solutions that can help people around the world.

Afnan Ashraf

Afnan Ashraf, aged 10+, is a bright and curious learner who loves all things tech. He completed his first web development course—covering HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap—at the Ali Jiwani Institute for Professional Development (IPD). With a keen eye for detail and a creative spark, Afnan helped design and build the Better Tomorrow website. He enjoys experimenting with code, learning how websites come together, and turning ideas into interactive pages. Afnan is passionate about using technology to make a difference and dreams of building tools and apps that improve lives and inspire others to explore the digital world.

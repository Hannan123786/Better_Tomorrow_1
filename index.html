About Us

The Ismaili Council for Pakistan serves as the primary planning and coordinating body for the Jamat in Pakistan. It operates through an extensive network of eight Regional Councils, each responsible for overseeing the needs and development of the Ismaili community in their respective areas. These Regional Councils include the Council for the Southern Region, the Council for the Central Region, the Council for the Hunza Region, the Council for the Gilgit Region, the Council for the Ishkoman/Puniyal Region, the Council for the Gupis/Yasin Region, the Council for the Lower Chitral Region, and the Council for the Upper Chitral Region. Together, these bodies ensure that the Jamat’s needs are addressed effectively across diverse geographic and cultural contexts.

Disaster Risk Reduction

We contributed in a project of CBDRM, which has been a cornerstone initiative of FOCUS Pakistan, now integrated into the Aga Khan Agency for Habitat (AKAH). This program is designed to empower local communities by equipping volunteers with the necessary skills and tools to act as first responders in the event of a disaster. By focusing on community-driven approaches, CBDRM ensures that those most affected by disasters are also the most prepared to handle them. This localized response is critical in minimizing the impact of disasters, as community volunteers are often the first to arrive at the scene and can provide immediate assistance before external help arrives...

Early warning saves lives

We provided a donation to CERT, which played a critical role in disaster response efforts during a devastating series of avalanches in Chitral in February 2017. This incident highlighted the importance of preparedness and community-based disaster management in saving lives and minimizing the impact of natural disasters. The heavy snowfall that preceded the avalanches caused widespread destruction, putting numerous villages at risk. However, the Early Warning Systems (EWS) established by FOCUS Pakistan proved to be a lifesaving intervention, demonstrating the value of proactive disaster risk management...
